## OUR LEADERSHIP TEAM

### Anthony Copping — FOUNDER & CEO
`images/anthony_new.png`

A serial entrepreneur, Anthony founded his first music publishing company at 21 and created Last Voices, a 20-year journey into the deepest jungles of the South Pacific, followed by National Geographic in a television series hailed as "the most dangerous journey ever undertaken in search of indigenous music." A composer and presenter, his work aired in over 100 countries, with albums released by Sony and Virgin.

He was an early investor in Totem, a video events technology business that was later acquired by FTSE 100 giant Informa. Binumi, his video technology platform with an exclusive 300,000 young people to create short documentaries. Today, Binumi AI empowers organisations to create purposeful, on-brand video at scale, turning everyday communication into stories that inspire, influence, and deliver real impact.

### Alexander Hasler — COO
`images/alexander.png`

Based in Switzerland, Alexander has joined Binumi in a dual role as Chief Operating Officer during the company's scale-up phase while also leading sales across the DACH region. As COO, he is responsible for building scalable operational structures, driving executional excellence, and supporting sustainable growth.

He brings extensive experience from senior roles at a global insurance group, combined with a strong track record in startup environments, enabling him to translate strategy into scalable operations and commercial success. In addition, Alexander serves as President of the donation-funded Green Ethiopia Foundation, supporting sustainable development and social impact initiatives.
